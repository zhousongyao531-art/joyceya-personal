AI visual system

Iris Intelligence

A speculative identity and interface language for AI-assisted insight discovery, built around the idea that perception can become a product capability.

Role

Visual designer, AI creator, frontend prototyper

Timeline

4 weeks

Tools

Figma, AI image tools, HTML/CSS/JS

Scope

Identity, visual language, landing concept, motion cues

Background

The project explores how an AI product can feel precise without becoming cold, using iris-inspired forms to connect machine analysis with human perception.

Design Goal

Create a premium visual system that communicates intelligence, trust, and discovery while remaining flexible enough for interface and campaign moments.

Visual Exploration

The exploration focused on circular signal fields, luminous gradients, dark glass surfaces, and restrained typography to balance technical depth with emotional clarity.

Final Design

The final direction combines an immersive hero, high-contrast hierarchy, refined motion, and modular content cards that can scale into product storytelling.

Reflection

The strongest opportunity is the relationship between data and atmosphere: visual systems can make complexity feel navigable, not merely impressive.
