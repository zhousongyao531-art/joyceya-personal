Branding

Living Brand Atlas

A modular identity direction for a culture-forward digital studio, designed to help the brand shift across editorial, social, and product contexts.

Role

Brand designer, visual strategist

Timeline

3 weeks

Tools

Figma, Illustrator, moodboard systems

Scope

Identity direction, art direction, brand guidelines

Background

The brand needed a flexible language that could feel strategic, editorial, and contemporary without losing recognizability.

Design Goal

Build a system with enough structure for consistency and enough movement for campaigns, partnerships, and evolving content themes.

Visual Exploration

Exploration centered on atlas-like layouts, soft geometric marks, layered color fields, and typography that feels crisp but warm.

Final Design

The final system uses modular compositions, a vivid accent palette, and clear brand rules for digital-first execution.

Reflection

A strong identity does not only decorate a message; it gives a team a repeatable way to think, decide, and publish.
