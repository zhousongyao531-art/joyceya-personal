Project Overview

Project Overview

Living Brand Atlas is a portfolio project page for selected brand and campaign planning decks. It focuses on how a brand challenge is translated into insight, concept, media rhythm, visual direction, and executable output.

Brand Challenge

The cases cover brands that needed clearer differentiation, stronger social memory, or a sharper route from attention to action across Xiaohongshu, IP co-branding, cultural tourism, travel, and lifestyle consumption.

Core Insight

A workable strategy starts by finding the user emotion behind the category: warmth, health, festival belonging, travel imagination, identity, or ritual moments.

Strategy

Each deck builds a planning path from audience definition and platform behavior to content themes, KOL/KOC logic, campaign nodes, and conversion touchpoints.

Visual Direction

The visual layer supports the strategic narrative through mood, rhythm, presentation hierarchy, campaign assets, and a consistent sense of brand atmosphere.

Outcome / Reflection

These works show my planning method: turn abstract brand goals into clear narrative systems, then connect them with content, media, visuals, and measurable actions.

项目概览

Living Brand Atlas 是用于展示品牌策划与 campaign 策略作品的项目页。重点呈现我如何把品牌命题拆解为洞察、概念、媒介节奏、视觉方向与可执行产出。

品牌挑战

这些案例分别面对品牌差异化、社交记忆点、内容声量到平台行动的转化问题，覆盖小红书种草、IP 联名、文旅节点、旅行平台与生活方式消费等场景。

核心洞察

有效策略通常来自对用户情绪的识别：保暖、安全感、健康、节日归属、旅行想象、身份表达或生活仪式感。

策略

每份方案都从人群定义、平台行为和传播目标出发，搭建内容主题、KOL/KOC 逻辑、活动节点与转化触点。

视觉方向

视觉部分服务于策略叙事，通过情绪色彩、版式节奏、提案层级、campaign 资产和统一的品牌氛围增强记忆点。

结果 / 反思

这些作品体现了我的策划方法：把抽象的品牌目标转化为清晰的叙事系统，再连接内容、媒介、视觉和可衡量的行动路径。

Xiaohongshu Node Marketing

Bananain Double 12 Campaign

Background

During the Double 12 shopping node, Bananain needed to strengthen winter-category awareness on Xiaohongshu and turn product features such as warmth, comfort, antibacterial fabric, and lightweight wear into searchable consumer language.

Requirement

The plan needed to build a KOL/KOC seeding matrix, clarify keyword deployment, and create a phased content rhythm that could support trust-building before conversion.

Idea

The creative route used winter warmth as the emotional entry point, connecting topics such as "winter warmth action" and product-specific keywords to repeated social exposure and user discussion.

Output

The deck delivered a Xiaohongshu campaign framework including pre-heating, influencer tiering, trial participation, keyword topics, content directions, and a node-based publishing strategy.

My Role

Chief Planner. I led the campaign logic, content structure, platform strategy, and KOL/KOC communication framework.

小红书节点营销

蕉内双十二营销方案

作品背景

在双十二消费节点下，蕉内需要在小红书强化冬季品类认知，将保暖、舒适、抗菌、轻薄等产品特点转化为用户更容易搜索和讨论的内容语言。

品牌需求

方案需要建立 KOL/KOC 种草矩阵，明确关键词投放路径，并形成从蓄势到转化的阶段性内容节奏。

核心想法

以“冬日温暖”为情绪入口，通过“冬日寻暖行动”等话题与产品关键词形成持续触达，让功能卖点被更自然地种草和记忆。

方案产出

最终产出包含小红书投放策略、达人分层、试用流程、话题矩阵、内容方向和双十二节点发布节奏。

我的角色

总策划。负责整体策略逻辑、内容结构、平台打法和 KOL/KOC 沟通框架设计。

IP Campaign Planning

Zero Point Idol: OUYE Coconut Milk

Background

The coconut beverage market is highly homogeneous, while younger consumers increasingly care about health, value, and emotional resonance. OUYE needed a differentiated concept to express its product strengths and younger brand personality.

Requirement

The brand needed a campaign concept that could communicate product benefits, build an IP image, and extend into online and offline scenarios.

Idea

The plan created the "Zero Point Idol" concept and personified the coconut ingredient as the IP character Ouo. The production process was translated into an idol-growth narrative from transformation to debut.

Output

The deck delivered IP design, stage-based campaign planning, social topic activations, festival collaboration ideas, offline interaction, documentary-style content, and live-stream concepts.

My Role

Chief Planner. I led the IP concept, campaign architecture, user scenario design, and online-offline activation ideas.

IP 营销策划

零分偶像：OU椰椰汁

作品背景

椰汁饮品市场同质化明显，而年轻消费者越来越重视健康、性价比和情绪价值。OU椰需要一个更有记忆点的概念来表达产品优势和年轻化品牌气质。

品牌需求

品牌需要一套既能讲清产品卖点，又能建立 IP 形象，并延展到线上线下场景的整合营销方案。

核心想法

方案提出“零分偶像”概念，将椰子原料拟人化为 IP 角色“欧欧”，并把产品生产过程转译为偶像养成叙事，从蜕变、成长到正式出道。

方案产出

最终产出包含 IP 形象设定、阶段式活动规划、社交话题、音乐节合作、线下互动、纪录片内容和直播间玩法。

我的角色

总策划。负责 IP 概念、活动架构、用户场景和线上线下联动创意设计。

Brand Image Campaign

Xiaochun Yingzi: Hidden Blue

Background

Xiaochun Yingzi needed to strengthen brand image around cakes, premium positioning, minimalist aesthetics, and healthier ingredients, while breaking the category stereotype of high sugar and heavy coloring.

Requirement

The campaign needed a memorable concept, a clearer target audience, and a coherent communication theme that could run through content, media, and KPI planning.

Idea

The plan built around the "Hidden Blue" concept, using a restrained emotional color cue to connect refined visual taste, ritual moments, and the lifestyle preferences of 24-35 year-old women.

Output

The deck delivered background review, TA insight, creative concept, communication strategy, budget thinking, KPI estimation, and an integrated campaign path for brand image enhancement.

My Role

Chief Planner. I led the insight framework, creative concept, campaign structure, and communication strategy.

品牌形象传播

小春英子：深藏 Blue

作品背景

小春英子希望围绕蛋糕品类强化品牌形象，突出中高端定位、简约美学和健康配料，同时打破用户对蛋糕“高热量、高色素”的固有印象。

品牌需要一个更有记忆点的传播概念、更清晰的目标人群，以及能够贯穿内容、媒介与 KPI 的整合传播主题。

核心想法

方案围绕“深藏 Blue”展开，用克制的蓝色情绪符号连接精致审美、仪式感场景和 24-35 岁女性用户的生活方式偏好。

方案产出

最终产出包含背景回顾、TA 洞察、创意概念、传播策略、预算思路、KPI 预估和品牌形象提升路径。

我的角色

总策划。负责洞察框架、创意概念、 campaign 结构和传播策略设计。

Planning Reflection

Across these cases, my planning approach focuses on turning brand goals into structured communication systems: defining the audience, finding the emotional entry point, building a campaign narrative, and translating strategy into executable media and content actions.
